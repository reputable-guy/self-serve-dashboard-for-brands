# Ideal First Customers for Reputable

*Research compiled January 2026*

## Executive Summary

This document identifies 10 ideal first customers for Reputable—DTC wellness/supplement brands in the $2M-50M revenue range that would benefit most from verified product studies with before/after data. These brands share common needs: proving efficacy for retail expansion, building investor confidence, and creating differentiated marketing content.

## 1. MUD\WTR

**Estimated revenue:** $30-50M (recently expanded to Costco Southeast and Target)

**Why they're a good fit:**
- Claims around "energy and focus without the jitters and crash" are perfect for a 28-day study
- Recently launched mushroom coffee with l-theanine - needs efficacy data to differentiate from competitors
- Expanding into major retail (Costco, Target, Sprouts) where clinical backing would give shelf advantage
- Founder Shane Heath is highly engaged and values scientific positioning

**Decision maker:** Shane Heath, Founder & CEO (very active on LinkedIn, shares product updates personally)

**Current approach to social proof:** Heavy reliance on founder storytelling, lifestyle content, and organic testimonials. No visible clinical studies or verified outcome data.

**Pain points that would resonate:**
- Differentiating from other mushroom coffee brands flooding the market
- Proving the "jitter-free energy" claim with real data
- Supporting retail buyer conversations with efficacy evidence

**How to reach them:**
- LinkedIn direct to Shane Heath (highly active, posts weekly)
- Email: team@mudwtr.com or via LinkedIn
- Industry events: Natural Products Expo West

## Outreach Strategy Summary

### Best Channels by Brand Type

| Brand Type | Primary Channel | Secondary Channel |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Founder-led (MUD\WTR, Moon Juice) | LinkedIn DM to founder | Instagram |
| Science-forward (Qualia, Performance Lab) | Email with research focus | LinkedIn |
| Retail-expanding (Cymbiotika, Beam) | Trade show introduction | LinkedIn |
| Tech-savvy (HigherDOSE) | PR/press introduction | Instagram |

### Key Conferences to Target
- **Natural Products Expo West** (March, Anaheim) - Most brands exhibit
- **Supply Side West** (November, Las Vegas) - Ingredient/science focus
- **Biohacking Conference** (Multiple) - Performance Lab, Qualia, LMNT audience

### Universal Pain Points (Use in Outreach)
1. **Retail expansion** - "Buyers want efficacy data before giving shelf space"
2. **Premium pricing justification** - "Your $50 product needs $50 worth of proof"
3. **Differentiation** - "Everyone claims their adaptogens work - prove yours do"
4. **Investor confidence** - "Series A/B rounds want clinical validation"
5. **Marketing content** - "Before/after data beats lifestyle photos"

### Pricing Positioning
- $2,500 for 50 participants = $50/participant
- Compare to: Clinical trial costs ($50K-500K), influencer campaigns ($5K-50K)
- ROI angle: "One verified study creates years of marketing content"
